Integrate and plots three 2-mode orbits in 4 different representation:

$ python ftwomode.py 


In order to generate gorbitsandslice figure, run:

$ bash gorbitsandslice.sh

This will generate the data in paraview/gorbitandslice

Then start paraview (version 4.1 is tested) and load state:

paraview/gorbitandslice/figstate.pvsm
