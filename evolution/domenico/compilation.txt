to compile and link to lapack

cc lapack_tut.c -L/usr/grads/lib -llapack -lblas -lgfortran

to compile and link to lapack with icc

icc -o renan renan_real.c -lm -llapack -lblas -lifcore 

to plot w/ gnuplot 

set term post enhanced monochrome

set output "lozi_eigenfunction.eps"

splot [-1:1][-1:1] "lozi_dens.dat" using 1:2:x

x is the (n+2)-th column of "lozi_dens.dat", n being the desired eigenmode (eigenvalues are tagged in "lozi_evs.dat")
